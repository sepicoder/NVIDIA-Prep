# NVIDIA Technical Program Manager Interview Preparation

## New Product Readiness | AI Infrastructure & Data Center Deployments

This repository contains my preparation materials for the NVIDIA Technical Program Manager role focused on New Product Readiness within Solutions Architecture.

The role sits at the intersection of program management, hyperscale AI infrastructure, customer engagement, product launch readiness, and large scale data center deployments. The objective is to ensure that NVIDIA's next generation platforms are operationally ready from engineering samples through mass production across cloud service provider (CSP) environments.

### Key Areas of Focus

#### AI Data Center Infrastructure

* High density AI and HPC data center architecture
* Power distribution and capacity planning
* Liquid and air cooling systems
* Rack level deployment strategies
* Hardware bring up and validation

#### Networking & Cluster Readiness

* InfiniBand and Ethernet networking
* Network provisioning and validation
* Cabling design and deployment planning
* Cluster scalability and performance considerations
* Operational readiness for large GPU deployments

#### Product Launch & Readiness

* Engineering sample to mass production lifecycle
* Readiness gates and launch criteria
* Documentation strategy
* Knowledge transfer and training programs
* Lessons learned capture and implementation

#### Technical Program Management

* Change management in rapidly evolving environments

#### Customer Readiness

* Customer engagement models

* Support and escalation planning

#### Quality & Continuous Improvement

Demonstrate the ability to lead cross functional programs that ensure NVIDIA products, infrastructure, documentation, tooling, processes, and customer support organizations are fully prepared for successful deployment at scale across the world's largest AI data centers.
